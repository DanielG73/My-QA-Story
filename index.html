About Me

My name is Daniel Gurevich, and I bring over 20 years of experience in computer repair and technical leadership. For most of my career, I managed computer labs and led technical teams, delivering fast and reliable solutions to hardware and software problems.

But even with all this hands-on experience, I realized I was missing something: a structured, forward-looking approach to software quality. That’s why I decided to study QA (Quality Assurance).

I’m now learning testing methodologies, test case design, and tools for both manual and automated testing.

My goal is to combine my deep technical background with modern QA practices — and bring real value to development teams and end users alike.
